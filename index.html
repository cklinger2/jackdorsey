Square

Twitter

  • 140 characters because Jack Dorsey wanted it to be people's thoughts and be personal and not overwhelm people.
  • Originally was going to be called "Twitch" but then a worker saw the word "twitter" in the dictionary, and the definition fit what they wanted the app to be.
  • Dorsey wanted everyone's Twitter account to be their own and to be different to each individual.
